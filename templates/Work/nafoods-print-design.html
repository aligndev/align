About Nafoods Group
Align worked with Nafoods Group to take over their print, packaging projects. We first designed event materials such as posters, standees, banners, and brochures. After that we help Nafoods launch their brand and packaging in the Russian market. The market has grown tremendously with many million dollars in growth after Align's help in the design of the packaging and print materials. Align also worked with Nafoods to help develop a new market in Vietnam to sell seeds, baby plants and fertilizers to Vietnamese farmers.
